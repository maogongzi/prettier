#### [HIGHLIGHT] Prevent wrapping object properties with short keys (#10335 by @thorn0)

Line breaks after short property names in object literals often look unnatural. Even when such a line break yields a line length benefit of 1 or 2 characters, it rarely looks justified. Prettier main avoids line breaks after property names shorter than `tabWidth + 3` â€“ for example, 5 characters in the default configuration, or 7 characters with `tabWidth: 4`. This heuristic may be revised in future versions.
